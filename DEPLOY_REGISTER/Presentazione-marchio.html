Financial Defense Shield

Institutional Presentation for Trademark Registration

Author: Attorney Marcello Stanca – Supreme Court Lawyer, President AMEV Firenze

1. Introduction

The Financial Defense Shield is an original figurative trademark representing a modern global system for protecting consumers from financial fraud. Its design incorporates a contemporary shield, an asymmetric profile inspired by legionary shields, concentric vigilance rings, and the signature M • © • S.

2. Blockchain-Based Proof of Authorship

The trademark has been minted as three NFTs on public blockchains (Polygon, Base, Celo), all publicly verifiable on-chain. The NFTs contain identical metadata to ensure evidentiary consistency, including the original high-resolution artwork and a cryptographic hash preventing unauthorized alteration.

3. Web Ecosystem and Use of the Trademark

The trademark is used across multilingual platforms such as tutelatruffe.it, scam-radar.com, tutelatruffe.fr, and tutelatruffe.de. These sites host one of the most accurate global databases of Financial Authorities and provide tools such as an interactive verification form and a European map of 50 regulators.

The flag-based search system creates a “chromatic Esperanto”, using national colors as a universal language to overcome linguistic barriers and make institutional access intuitive and inclusive.

4. Psychological Self-Defense

The ecosystem introduces psychological self-defense as the first barrier against fraud, offering emotional vulnerability self-assessment tools in ten languages—addressing a gap left by traditional MIFID questionnaires.

5. European Petitions and Institutional Impact

Petitions 0888/2024 and 0645/2025, authored by Attorney Stanca, were accepted and commended by the European Parliament, contributing to the Agreement of 27 November 2025 establishing strict liability for banks, PSPs, and major web platforms that fail to block fraudulent content.

6. Active Participation and Gamification

Users are encouraged to become active defenders—symbolically acting as Caesar’s legionaries or companions of Ulysses— transforming financial defense into an empowering, narrative-driven experience.

7. Public Recognition

Attorney Stanca is also the author of the Amazon Best Seller “Rischio Trading”, reinforcing the authority and public relevance of the Financial Defense Shield initiative.

8. Conclusion

The Financial Defense Shield trademark is supported by blockchain evidence, international usage, institutional reforms, and a comprehensive consumer protection ecosystem—representing a unique contribution to global financial safety.
