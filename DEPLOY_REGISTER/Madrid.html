Financial Defense Shield

Madrid System – Trademark Presentation

Mark Type: Figurative Trademark

Applicant: Attorney Marcello Stanca – Supreme Court Lawyer, President AMEV Firenze

1. Description of the Mark

The Financial Defense Shield is an original figurative trademark consisting of:

  • a modern protective shield with an asymmetric upper profile inspired by historical legionary shields,
  • concentric yellow vigilance rings symbolizing alertness and security,
  • the integrated signature M • © • S.

The mark visually represents vigilance, integrity, and consumer protection in the financial sector.

2. Basis of Authorship and Priority (Blockchain Evidence)

Authorship and priority are supported by blockchain-based evidence. The original high-resolution artwork of the figurative trademark has been minted as three Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) on public, decentralized, Ethereum-compatible blockchains:

  • Polygon PoS
  • Base (Ethereum Layer 2)
  • Celo Network

All records are publicly verifiable on-chain. The three NFTs contain identical metadata to ensure consistency of evidence, including:

  • the original high-resolution artwork of the figurative trademark,
  • descriptive metadata,
  • a cryptographic hash that guarantees the artwork cannot be altered without invalidating the blockchain record.

3. Use in Commerce and International Web Presence

The mark is used across multilingual consumer-protection platforms, including:

  • www.tutelatruffe.it
  • www.scam-radar.com
  • www.tutelatruffe.fr
  • www.tutelatruffe.de

These websites host one of the most accurate global databases of Financial Authorities and provide:

  • an interactive form for verifying the authenticity of financial operators,
  • a European interactive map of 50 Financial Authorities,
  • a flag-based search interface described as a “chromatic Esperanto”, using national flags and colors as a universal visual language to overcome linguistic and etymological barriers.

The mark is consistently used as a symbol of reliability, vigilance, and consumer protection within this ecosystem.

4. Distinctiveness

The Financial Defense Shield is distinctive due to:

  • its unique artistic composition and symbolic elements,
  • its non-descriptive nature with respect to the services offered,
  • its consistent use on international consumer-protection platforms,
  • its association with a structured ecosystem dedicated to financial safety and fraud prevention.

5. Supporting Background

The applicant, Attorney Marcello Stanca, is the author of European Parliament Petitions 0888/2024 and 0645/2025, both accepted and commended, which contributed to the Agreement of 27 November 2025 on strict liability for banks, payment service providers and major web platforms in relation to fraudulent content.

He is also the author of the Amazon Best Seller “Rischio Trading”, a practical manual on financial self-defense against online trading scams, reinforcing his authority in this field.

6. Conclusion

The Financial Defense Shield is an original figurative trademark supported by blockchain-based immutable authorship evidence and consistent international use. It is suitable for protection under the Madrid System and for inclusion in the WIPO Global Brand Database as a distinctive mark associated with consumer protection and financial safety.
